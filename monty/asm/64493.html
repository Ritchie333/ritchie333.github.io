FBED: Fill a sprite with an attribute
Used by the routines at 8866 and FBB4.

L X co-ordinate

FBF1 LD (HL),A
FBF2 INC HL
FBF3 LD (HL),A

FBF7 ADD HL,DE
FBF8 LD (HL),A Put the attribute in the next two columns.
FBF9 INC HL
FBFA LD (HL),A
